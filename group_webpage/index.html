Team Ducks: Our Vision

We are the Ducks, a team of passionate computer scientists dedicated to delivering DuckDash, an Online Restaurant Ordering System (OROS). Our goal is to simplify the food ordering process for customers and restaurants by creating a high-quality, user-friendly platform. We value collaboration, innovation, and efficiency, and we apply Scrum principles to ensure a successful project outcome.

Team Rules

  • Commitment: Participate in daily stand-ups at 10 AM and sprint planning each Monday. Meet sprint deadlines for assigned tasks.
  • Courage: Offer honest feedback during retrospectives and suggest improvements. Take initiative to tackle challenges and propose solutions.
  • Focus: Prioritize sprint goals and minimize unrelated activities. Keep non-essential communication to a minimum.
  • Openness: Share updates and obstacles during stand-ups for transparency. Promote collaborative dialogue to address issues.
  • Respect: Appreciate diverse viewpoints and provide constructive input. Ensure every team member has a voice in discussions.
